The Best Movies According to Wex

My top 3 movies of all-time.

REDLINE

Redline is about the biggest and most deadly racing tournament in the universe.

The animation of this movie is absolutely breathtaking being produced by the legendary studio MADHOUSE.

Koe no Katachi (A Silent Voice)

A very beautiful story about finding yourself and accepting the people around you.

Baby Driver

A thrilling movie with the most spectacular opening I have ever seen.

If you love cars, action and a bit of romance I suggest giving it a watch.
